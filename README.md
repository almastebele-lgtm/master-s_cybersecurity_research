# master-s_cybersecurity_research
This is an executive summary of my research based on my third year university internship at CERT.LV. It is an introduction to a presentation I delivered to the examination jury. It investigates the topic of why, if cyberciminals are becoming increasingly more persistent, creative, and diverse, is cyberliteracy stagnating in the EU? The views and observations expressed in this document are my own and do not represent the views of CERT.LV or any other organisation I have been affiliated with.
